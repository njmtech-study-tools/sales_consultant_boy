01

Who is the customer?

The primary customers are Sales Consultants working in retail, service-based businesses, or B2B environments. These are individuals responsible for engaging customers, answering questions, and closing deals.

02

Who is affected?

Stakeholders

  • Sales Consultants — direct users of the tool
  • Sales Managers — performance tracking & revenue oversight
  • Customers — end-users receiving faster, better service

03

Customer problem or opportunity

Sales Consultants often:

  • Struggle to remember detailed product information
  • Take too long to respond to customer queries
  • Miss sales opportunities due to lack of real-time insights
  • Deliver inconsistent messaging across customers

Opportunity

Improve sales performance by giving consultants instant, intelligent support during every customer interaction.

04

What are you proposing to launch?

A Sales Assistant Chatbot powered by AI — acting as a real-time assistant that helps Sales Consultants:

  • Answer customer questions instantly
  • Recommend products and services
  • Handle objections confidently
  • Generate persuasive, on-brand responses

05

Why does the customer need this?

For Sales Consultants

  • Sell faster and more confidently
  • Reduce mental load
  • Provide accurate, consistent information
  • Increase conversion rates and commissions

For Businesses

  • Improved customer experience
  • Increased revenue
  • Reduced training time for new hires

06

How does it work?

The chatbot operates as an AI-powered assistant integrated into the sales workflow:

1

Input

The Sales Consultant inputs a customer question or scenario — e.g., "Customer wants a cheaper alternative."

2

Processing

The chatbot processes the request using trained data: product knowledge, sales scripts, and customer behaviour patterns.

3

Response Generation

It generates a smart response — a suggested reply, product recommendation, or upsell / cross-sell strategy.

4

Real-Time Action

The consultant uses or adapts the response in real-time during the customer interaction.
